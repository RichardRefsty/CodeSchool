About : Coding School

Coding School is a computer programming site created and maintained by the students of Graphic Era Hill University ,Bhimtal. Our vision for this site is to share our passion for coding through a series of interactive programming challenges, as well as offering tutorials in (C / C++ / Python ). Whether you are an experienced programmer looking for a challenge, a beginner aiming to better understand your coursework, or anywhere in between, this site has something for you.
Welcome and happy coding!
